Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is this app?

Our app is a hidden, password-protected gallery designed to securely store your confidential photos, documents, and passwords. It disguises itself as a calculator to protect your privacy.

2. How do I access my hidden gallery?

To access your hidden gallery, open the app and enter your secret code in the calculator interface. This will unlock the gallery where your confidential files are stored.

3. How do I set my secret code?

During the initial setup, you will be prompted to create a secret code. If you need to change it later, go to the app settings and select "Change Password".

4. What features are available in the free version?

The free version of the app includes:

  • Basic storage for photos and documents
  • Calculator disguise interface
  • Basic security features

5. What additional features do I get with a subscription?

Subscribing to our premium service unlocks the following features:

  • Ad-free experience
  • Unlimited storage capacity
  • Custom albums
  • New updates

6. How do I subscribe to the premium service?

To subscribe, open the app, go to the settings menu, and select "Upgrade to Premium". Follow the prompts to complete your subscription.

7. How can I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, open the app, go to the settings menu, and select "Request cancel or refund".

8. How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, open the app, go to the settings menu, and select "Request cancel or refund".

9. Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is securely encrypted and stored. We use advanced encryption methods to ensure your files are protected from unauthorized access.

10. How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, please email us at huseyin2demir@outlook.com. We are here to help you 24/7.

11. Can I recover deleted files?

Deleted files are permanently removed and cannot be recovered. Please ensure you have backups of important files before deleting them.

12. Can I secure my documents with this app?

Yes, you can securely store documents in the app. Simply upload photos of your documents to the app, and they will be protected with the same encryption as your other photos.

13. How do I create custom albums?

To create custom albums, go to the gallery view, select "Create Album" and name your new album. You can then add photos and documents to this album for better organization.

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
